Three Pillars of Truth

MATTHEW 5:17-18
“Do not think that I have come to abolish the Law or the Prophets; I have not come to abolish them but to fulfill them. For truly I tell you, until heaven and earth disappear, not the smallest letter, not the least stroke of a pen, will by any means disappear from the Law until everything is accomplished.”
Lesson: Christ came to fulfill God's perfect law. His grace doesn't erase truth—it completes it. Let this anchor your faith.
MATTHEW 5:7
“Blessed are the merciful, for they will be shown mercy.”
Mercy reflection: The mercy we extend echoes heaven's mercy toward us. Forgiveness breaks chains.
1 PETER 5:8
“Be alert and of sober mind. Your enemy the devil prowls around like a roaring lion looking for someone to devour.”

⚡ MORAL OF THE VERSE: Always be alert because Satan can just come and attack you anytime. Stay watchful, prayerful, and grounded in God's Word. Resist him, standing firm in the faith (1 Peter 5:9).

“Resist him, standing firm in the faith, because you know that the family of believers throughout the world is undergoing the same kind of sufferings.”

How to Share Your Faith (Practical)

1. Start with prayer — ask God for boldness. 2. Share your personal testimony of God's goodness. 3. Use scripture like John 3:16 or Romans 10:9. 4. Listen with love and patience. Our “Sharing Your Faith” lesson dives into real-life conversation models.

1 Corinthians 13 — The Love Chapter

“Love is patient, love is kind... It does not dishonor others, it is not self-seeking...” This lesson will teach you all about love and how to be like Jesus. Faith, hope, and love abide; the greatest of these is love. Let love lead every interaction.
